APAP, a novel shape deformation method that results in plausible and realistic deformed mesh, that cannot be achieved by previous mesh deformation method, while enabling the preservation of fine details from the original mesh through the Jacobian fields and the Score Distillation Sampling loss. Given an 2D image or 3D mesh, the user clicks a handle point(\Red{red}) and a target point(\Blue{blue}).
